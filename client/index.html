About Us

YUJ is the official Yoga Club of VIT, Pune, established in January 2021, focuses on the physical and mental growth of one and all. We at YUJ spread awareness among people about yoga, its benefits, and how to lead a healthy life.We conduct workshops in order to aware people about the benifits of yoga in today's hustling life .We have organised yoga sessions during lockdowns also in online mode successfully .Our team will reach maximum people through the workshops ,session to make Yoga as a 'practice' in their routine.
